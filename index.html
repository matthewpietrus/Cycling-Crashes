The COVID-19 pandemic has caused a surge in cycling within NYC. With more New Yorkers commuting, exercising, or simply joy-riding throughout the city, there have inevitably been more accidents. This map shows the more serious of these accidents, where two or more cyclists were injured since January 2020.
